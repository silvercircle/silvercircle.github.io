This is an archived category of old posts (some as old as 13 years) about an Open Source project I was once working on. Tabsrmm is a part of the multiprotocol instant messaging client Miranda IM that was once very poplar during the golden era of instant messaging (10-15 years ago, before social networks came into existence).

While I do no longer maintain any code related to Miranda IM and the program itself lost a significant part of its once huge user- and developer base during the rise of social networks and mobile communication, it is still around as an active Open Source project.

See the list of archived articles here.
