## Things we know about Bran

Time travel has traditionally been a science fiction thing, but it has been used in fantasy before.
</div>
* he is not the common warg as his abilities are far stronger and much more powerful. For example, 
  Bran has no problems to warg into humans (which is very special and impossible for most wargs) 
  *and* he can actually take influence on the past. We have seen this on at least two occasions:
  * The famous Hodor scene that explains how the stable boy became Hodor. It's a classical time (or 
    temporal) paradox, in which an action from an actor alters something in the past, which in turn 
    has influence on everything that follows.
  * When Bran calls after his father Ned at the famous Tower of Joy scene in episode 3 of the sixth 
    season, Ned actually reacts as if he had heard the call.
  This ability to effect the past is very important for this theory.
